Replaceable Images

  • Image`--match-image` controls the full-frame court/photo layer.
  • ImagePortraits are `img` slots with fixed safe areas.
  • ImageAvatar and sponsor backgrounds are separate slots, not baked into UI.

Interface Pieces

  • UIScorebug, rails, stat rows, footers, and social strips are CSS/HTML.
  • UIFlags and logos use local SVG assets inside clipped wrappers.
  • UIClean previews are separate from explanatory annotation text.
